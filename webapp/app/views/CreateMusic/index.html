Record Music

You don't need a full blown recording studio these days to make professional sounding productions.

The 9dials studio is a free online digital audio workstation aimed at making recording your tracks as simple and fun as possible.

While still under construction, the studio will provide all of the functionality you would expect from a desktop DAW, with all the added advantages of being online.
